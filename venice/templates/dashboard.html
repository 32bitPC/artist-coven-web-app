1. History

What is Artist Coven ?

Artist Coven is a group chat that originally was created exclusively for artists in Roblox group.

Prior to April 17, Artist Coven was called "Roblox Artists". Since then the name was changed to "Artist Coven" due to it has no longer associated with the main Roblox group.
